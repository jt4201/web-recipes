  meal comes together without any fuss.

\sbox{\myIngred}{
  \begin{minipage}[t]{\the\dimexpr0.35\textwidth}
    \textbf{Ingredients}
    \begin{itemize}
      \item 2 tablespoons olive oil
      \item 1 lemon juiced
      \item 1 tablespoon dried oregano
      \item ½ teaspoon garlic powder
      \item ½ teaspoon salt
      \item 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1-inch pieces
      \item 1 small zucchini sliced into ¼-inch moons
      \item 1 red onion chopped
      \item 14 cherry tomatoes
      \item ½ cup prepared tzatziki store-bought or recipe as follows
      \item Tzatziki Sauce
        \begin{itemize}
          \item ½ cup Greek yogurt
          \item ¼ cucumber seeds removed \& chopped fine
          \item 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
          \item 2 cloves garlic finely minced
          \item ¼ teaspoon salt
        \end{itemize}
    \end{itemize}
  \end{minipage}
}

\sbox{\myMethod}{
  \begin{minipage}[t]{\the\dimexpr0.6\textwidth-2em}
    \textbf{Method}
    \begin{enumerate}
      \item Mix the olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, garlic powder, and ½ teaspoon
        salt together in a bowl. Add the chicken and toss to coat. Set aside for 30
        minutes (up to 2 hours) to marinate, tossing occasionally.
      \item To make the tzatziki (can be made ahead):
        \begin{enumerate}
          \item Remove excess moisture from the chopped cucumber by blotting with a towel.
            Place the cucumber, Greek yogurt, mint, garlic, and salt in a small bowl and
            mix to combine.
          \item Cover \& store in your cooler if making ahead of time.
        \end{enumerate}
      \item Prepare a grill or campfire for grilling.
      \item Thread the chicken and vegetables onto skewers.
      \item Once the grill is ready, place the skewers on the grill grate and grill,
        turning every few minutes so all sides cook evenly. The chicken will need to
        cook 3--5 minutes per side, and the veggies will need roughly 10--12 minutes.
      \item Remove skewers. Serve with tzatziki for dipping. Enjoy!
    \end{enumerate}
  \end{minipage}
}
